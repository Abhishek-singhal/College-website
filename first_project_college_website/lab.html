Physics & Electronics lab....

The campus has one electronics cum physics lab , fully air conditioned. The lab is equipped with the latest instruments needed for experiment.

Computer lab

The campus has two computer labs, fully air conditioned. One for seniors(both 2nd and 3rd year) and one for juniours. For the first year one pc is shared between two persons. Internet facility is quite good at labs. The lab timings are from 9a.m. to 12p.m.
