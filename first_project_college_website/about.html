The campus was founded in year 2005 as an initiative to provide quality education in Uttar Pradesh by government of India. It is an extension campus of IIIT-Allahabad. In fourth year, the students are transferred to the main campus IIIT-Allahabad for the placements.

The campus is located in Teekarmafi near an ashram and is surrounded by wild jungle and a small flowing river. It is 10 km away from main city Amethi and 31 km away from Sultanpur and around 50 km away from Pratapgarh.
